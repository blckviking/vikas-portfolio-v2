05 · The Market Opportunity

Our addressable TAM$100M+

The same platform.
A whole new world.

Industrial process safety across SEA is underserved and overwhelmingly manual. Incumbents sell bloated on-premise systems. We offered a modern, cloud-native, mobile-first, AI-run alternative.

Our existing telematics infrastructure, alert engine, and compliance module covered ~70% of what industrial clients needed out of the box. The remaining 30% was net-new: equipment-specific sensor ingestion, the AI layer, and industrial compliance templates.

Once we saw the reuse ratio, the business case wrote itself.
